This Program is a Simple Car Game.
This is the first prototype. You are competing against a Machine Learning Car with the same speed.
This car operates by utilizing a Feed Forward algorithm for it's neural network.
Since it is just a simple game to release stress your controls are the ARROW keys on your computer keyboard
UP key moves car forward
DOWN key is reverse
RIGHT key is right
LEFT key is left
Your Mission is to bypass other cars without colliding with any car or outer road borders.
This is LEVEL ONE with only 20 cars to bypass
The AI car is BLUE. Your car is PINK.
If you collide GAME OVER. Press on the Reload button to start again.
